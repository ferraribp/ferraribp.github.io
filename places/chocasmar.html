Travel to Chocas Mar

If you want to go to the beach in northern Mozambique then you should go to Chocas Mar.

Reasons to go

  • there are clean beaches
  • there are nice places to stay
  • there is good food

Places to stay

  1. Coral Lodge
  2. Feitoria
  3. Chocas Mar
  4. Oasis Mar e Sol
  5. Carrusca Mar e Sol
